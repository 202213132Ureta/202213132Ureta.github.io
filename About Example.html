About Us

Koi Koi Burgers was built with the intention of providing quality products at a low price. It started in San Marcelino St. near Adamson University in 2019. Koi Koi Burgers provided quality burgers for the students at Adamson University at an affordable price. Because of this Koi Koi Burgers became very popular among the students, so popular in fact that the owner decided to expand so that they can provide their products not only to students, but to the entire world starting here in the Philippines. Get Your Burgers Now At Koi Koi Burgers!!

Services

Koi Koi Burgers provides the classic fast food experience with gourmet techniques at a affordable price. Our ingredients is top notch and guaranteed fresh, we source our ingredients locally. We make our burgers when ordered, usually this method takes time, but here at Koi Koi Burgers, we ensure that you will receive your order within 3-5 minutes as we have built a quick and easy system in our restaurants that make it so that customers do not have to wait as long for a quality product. We believe that quick execution is just as important as a quality product and the system we have in place ensures both of that.

Location

The Main Branch of Koi Koi Burgers is located at UN Square in San Marcelino, Near Adamson University. We intended to sell our products to mainly students at first, but as our popularity grew, our customer pool has expanded. As we build new branches of Koi Koi Burgers, we intend to follow the same business model of selling mainly to students at first and then we expanding our customer pool. Soon Koi Koi Burgers will be at most universities in the country.
